Sam Altman

Entrepreneur, Investor, and AI Visionary

Biography

Born on April 22, 1985, in St. Louis, Missouri, Sam Altman showed an early interest in technology, beginning to code at the age of eight. His passion for computers led him to Stanford University, where he studied Computer Science, though he eventually made the decision to drop out to pursue entrepreneurial ventures.

In 2005, at just 19 years old, Altman founded Loopt, a pioneering location-based social networking app that allowed users to share their locations with friends. The company was ahead of its time, predating the widespread adoption of smartphones. After several years of growth and development, Loopt was acquired by Green Dot Corporation in 2012 for $43.4 million.

On the personal front, Sam married Oliver Mulherin in January 2024 in a private ceremony. The couple welcomed their first child, a son, in February 2025, marking a new chapter in Altman's life beyond his professional achievements.

Throughout his career, Altman has been known for his forward-thinking approach to technology and business, as well as his commitment to ensuring that technological advancements benefit humanity as a whole. His journey from a young programmer to one of the most influential figures in the tech industry exemplifies his vision, determination, and innovative spirit.

Career Milestones

Founding of Loopt

2005

Founded Loopt, a location-based social networking app, at age 19 while at Stanford University.

Loopt Acquisition

2012

Loopt acquired by Green Dot Corporation for $43.4 million.

Y Combinator

2011-2014

Joined Y Combinator as a part-time partner in 2011, later becoming president in 2014.

Co-founding OpenAI

2015

Co-founded OpenAI as a non-profit AI research laboratory with the mission to ensure AGI benefits all of humanity.

OpenAI CEO

2019

Became CEO of OpenAI, leading the organization's transition to a "capped-profit" model.

Worldcoin & Tools For Humanity

2019

Co-founded Tools For Humanity, the company behind Worldcoin, a cryptocurrency project.

Brief Ousting & Reinstatement

November 2023

Briefly removed as OpenAI CEO before being reinstated following significant company and public support.

Philosophy & Vision

🌐

AGI for Humanity

Sam Altman envisions a future where Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity. He believes that AGI should be developed in a way that is safe, broadly distributed, and aligned with human values.

⚖️

Progressive Policies

Altman advocates for progressive economic policies such as Universal Basic Income (UBI) and land value taxation. He sees these as potential solutions to address economic inequality, especially in a future where AI might disrupt traditional employment.

🛡️

AI Safety & Regulation

While pushing the boundaries of AI development, Altman emphasizes the importance of AI safety and appropriate regulation. He has engaged with policymakers globally to help shape responsible AI governance while balancing innovation.
